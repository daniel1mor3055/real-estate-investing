# Real Estate Investment Analyzer — For Mortgage Advisors

## What Is This Tool?

A professional-grade **rental property investment analysis engine** that evaluates deals end-to-end — from loan structuring through multi-year cash flow projections, sensitivity analysis, and scenario stress-testing.

It natively supports **Israeli mortgage structures** (multi-track, CPI-linked, Bank of Israel compliance) alongside conventional US-style financing.

---

## Why Should a Mortgage Advisor Care?

### 1. Close More Deals by Showing Clients the Numbers

Most buyers hesitate because they can't *see* how a deal plays out. This tool generates a full investment picture in seconds:

- **Year-by-year pro-forma**: NOI, cash flow, property value, equity buildup, ROE — for any holding period
- **Key metrics at a glance**: Cap Rate, Cash-on-Cash, DSCR, IRR, NPV, Equity Multiple, Break-Even Ratio, GRM
- **Monthly amortization schedules** per loan track and combined

> Instead of saying "trust me, this is a good deal" — you hand the client a professional report with the math behind it.

### 2. Compare Financing Options Side-by-Side

The tool's **financing comparison** feature lets you model multiple loan structures and instantly show clients which one produces the best returns:

- Fixed vs. variable rate tracks
- Different down payment percentages
- Short-term vs. long-term loans
- Mixed multi-track Israeli mortgages (קל"צ, פריים, צמוד מדד, משתנה)

> You become the advisor who doesn't just offer a mortgage — you prove *which* mortgage maximizes the client's wealth.

### 3. Israeli Mortgage Engine — A Unique Edge

No off-the-shelf tool handles Israeli mortgage complexity like this:

- **Up to 3 tracks** with independent types, rates, and terms
- **CPI-linked principal** calculations with expected inflation adjustments
- **Grace periods** (interest-only or full deferral)
- **Scheduled rate changes** and **prepayment events** (reduce term or reduce payment)
- **Bank of Israel compliance** checks (≥1/3 fixed, ≤2/3 variable)
- **Repayment methods**: Spitzer (annuity), Equal Principal, Bullet

> Clients shopping for Israeli mortgages will trust the advisor who can model their exact structure — not a rough estimate.

### 4. Sensitivity & Scenario Analysis — Handle Objections Before They Come Up

Clients worry: "What if rates go up? What if rents drop? What if vacancy spikes?"

This tool answers all of those with:

- **2D sensitivity heatmaps**: Pick any two variables (interest rate, rent, vacancy, appreciation, purchase price, etc.) and any target metric — see how the deal performs across a range of scenarios
- **Pessimistic / Base / Optimistic scenario comparison**: Pre-built stress tests that adjust appreciation, rent growth, expense growth, vacancy, and rates simultaneously
- **Break-even analysis**: What's the minimum rent for positive cash flow? What's the maximum purchase price for a target return?

> You de-risk the conversation. Clients see the worst case, the base case, and the best case — and they still want to move forward.

## What the Tool Calculates

| Metric | What It Tells the Client |
|--------|-------------------------|
| **NOI** (Net Operating Income) | Annual profit before debt service |
| **Cap Rate** | Return on the property itself, independent of financing |
| **Cash-on-Cash Return** | Return on the cash they actually invested |
| **DSCR** (Debt Service Coverage) | Can the property cover its loan payments? |
| **IRR** (Internal Rate of Return) | True annualized return including appreciation and exit |
| **NPV** (Net Present Value) | Is the deal worth more than the money invested? |
| **Equity Multiple** | Total wealth generated per dollar invested |
| **Break-Even Ratio** | How much income goes to expenses + debt |
| **GRM** (Gross Rent Multiplier) | Quick purchase price vs. rent sanity check |
| **ROE** (Return on Equity) | How hard the equity is working year over year |
